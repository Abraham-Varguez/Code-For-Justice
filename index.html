Welcome to Mending Minds!

We are a non-profit organization committed to providing free mental health services to marginalized communities. Our mission is to break down the barriers that prevent individuals and families from accessing quality mental health care. We believe that everyone deserves to receive the care and support they need to live healthy, fulfilling lives.

Where

to Find US

Mending Minds is proud to partner with multiple hospitals across the United States to provide the best possible care for our clients. Our partner hospitals are located in all 50 states, so no matter where you live, you can access our services.

Our Team

Our team of mental health professionals includes licensed therapists, counselors, and social workers who are committed to providing evidence-based treatments and therapies. We offer a range of services, including individual counseling, group therapy, family therapy, and more. All of our services are provided completely free of charge, thanks to the generous support of donors and volunteers.

Our Promise

At Mending Minds, your health and well-being are our top priority. We understand that mental health is a crucial part of overall health, and we're committed to providing high-quality, evidence-based mental health services to help you achieve your best possible outcomes.
